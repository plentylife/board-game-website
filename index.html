Everyone is a co-op

We simply forget about it. We have not been taught how to join together, and get sh*t done. We don't see ourselves as powerful agents in the grander economy.

The truth is: we are powerful, we can work together and achieve a comfortable life for everybody. We call ourselves "Plenty of Thanks". Our motto is "Plenty for all"!

How much say do you have?

Your life depends on hundreds if not thousands of brands; some you are not even aware of. Yet, how much say do you have?

For the majority, the answer is: from little to none. We, on the other hand, offer a way for grass-roots and cooperatives to involve the communities around them at a massive scale, where anyone has the opportunity to be directly involved.

Plenty of Thanks

We are the little heroes trying to slay the giant beast of our insatiable consumerist economy. To aid us in this quest, we developed a method we call "Corporation on a Board" that can transform even a group of strangers into a sustainable business. 

Our current focus is to solve every co-op's and not-for-profit's worst nightmare: lack of funding and lack of support. As a fellow not-for-profit, trust us, we know it hurts.

We are currently running pilot projects in South-Western Ontario; if you would like to join or find out more email join@plentyofthanks.ca
